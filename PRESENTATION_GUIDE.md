## 💡 PRO TIPS FOR YOUR PRESENTATION

### Before You Start
- ✅ Practice with a timer — do 10+ rehearsals
- ✅ Have your agent running and ready to demo
- ✅ Open dashboard.html in browser beforehand
- ✅ Test your demo setup — nothing kills momentum like tech issues

### During the Demo
- ✅ **Energy up!** Smile, gesture, show excitement
- ✅ Point at the screen as you explain each section
- ✅ Use phrases like "Watch this" and "Here's the magic"
- ✅ If demo hiccups, have screenshots ready as backup

### What to CUT if running short
- Detailed explanation of .env setup (not relevant for 3-min pitch)
- Troubleshooting section
- Technical implementation details
- Code examples

### What to EMPHASIZE
- The **problem first** (relatable to all devs)
- The **autonomy** (agent works without you)
- The **numbers** (hours saved = impact)
- The **visual workflow** (Think-Decide-Act)
